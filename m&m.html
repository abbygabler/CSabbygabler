    Data Collection

    We collected our data by licking over 100 M and M's and recording how long it took to get to the center, how hany licks it took to get to the center, and the color of the M and M's.


    Data Analysis

    We fount that, through the power of messing with google sheets for about a half an hour, that on average the green M and M's were the easiest to lick, they took an average 42 licks and 15 seconds, compared to the red M and M's, which took 48 licks over 18 seconds.
